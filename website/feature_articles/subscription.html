OsmAnd Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions for Android version of OsmAnd
  • Subscriptions for iOS version of OsmAnd

OsmAnd app is available for Android and iOS systems. There is a great variety of features that OsmAnd offers. Most of them are available in a free version of the application. The paid OsmAnd services provide extended functionality.

Subscriptions for Android version of OsmAnd

OsmAnd application has many features and tools. Of course, most of them are free.

Now, OsmAnd has two general subscription plans: Maps+ (yearly) and OsmAnd Pro (monthly / yearly).

The table from OsmAnd Docs shows all the features that are included in the paid and free packages of OsmAnd.

Subscriptions costs are shown in OsmAnd Docs.

You can check your purchases: the main menu -> Settings -> Purchases.

New subscriptions (OsmAnd Pro and OsmAnd Maps+) became active from OsmAnd 4.0 (August 2021). All earlier subscriptions (OsmAnd Live) are legacy and technically equal to "OsmAnd Maps+ subscription", though legacy subscriptions are entitled to Hourly Map updates. Users can't buy legacy subscriptions but old subscriptions are automatically renewed until the user unsubscribes (the price for them might change in the future).

Subscriptions for iOS version of OsmAnd

The OsmAnd Live subscription for iOS has three periods: monthly, three months, yearly. This feature allows you to get the unlimited download of all maps and get all the plug-ins for the subscription period. You also get the opportunity to frequently update maps.

Subscriptions costs are shown in OsmAnd Docs.

To get this feature, go to the main menu-> Maps & Resources-> OsmAnd Live -> Get OsmAnd Live. You can stop the subscription at any time using the settings in your iTunes account.
